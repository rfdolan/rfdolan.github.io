Arkats (ARE-cots)

The continent that the adventure takes place on.

  • Arevelk

    • Eastern part of Arkats. Held by the Brightarrows.

    • Marshview

      • Town in the east near a swamp. Nearby is the abandoned fort where our heroes found Long-Tassles' note.

      • The Flying Jellyfish - Tavern
    • Bogwater

      • Lakeside town and home of Callus Cold.

      • Stanley's Spear Shoppe
    • Halfhome

      • Town on a flat plain surrounded by walls. Contains a nice library and blacksmith, as well as a fountain in the middle of town.

      • The Old Halfhome Library
      • Cork's Smithery
    • Westfront

      • War camp/ small town near The Divide.

      • Samuel Sevenstrings' War Tent
      • Southern fortress (~2hrs south)
      • Long-Tassles' Tomb (~1hr north)
    • Skullview

      • Town a day's ride from Westfront. Was overrun with zombies controlled by a mind flayer. Has a large temple and a well.

    • Kellasburg (Click for map!)

      • Home of the adventurer Kella, who has a statue in the town square. Whenever someone dies here the stonemasons race to create a statue of them, as the best and quickest statue is rewarded with 250GP.

      • Chaz Razmian's Gallery of Wonders
    • Abandoned Temple to Tarragon

      • Temple of Tarragon which was abandoned and taken over by some strange cultists.

    • The Triplets

      • Northern port of Arevelk and setting off point for many great pirates.

    • Avazi: The City of Sand

      • Capital of Arevelk.

  • Mayramoot

    • Western part of Arkats. Held by The Masons.

    • Grey Stump

      • Town at the summit of a short mountain. Home to the ginger merchant and Lia's Tavern.

    • Jungle's Edge
    • Menak
    • Misthold

      • Military capital of Mayramoot.

    • Voch
    • Mount Memory
    • Katar
  • The Wastes

    • Frozen, barren lands that are rarely explored by residents of the rest of Arevelk.

  • The Divide

    • A large chasm seperatin Arevelk and Mayramoot. Nobody knows exactly how it came to be or what is at the bottom.

    • Dryport

      • A contested port south of The Divide. Even it is split in half.
