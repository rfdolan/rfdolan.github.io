ABOUT

Hi, my name is Raymond Dolan! I was born and raised in the great little state of Rhode Island. Ever since I was little I enjoyed tinkering with computers and solving interesting problems. That naturally led me to the wonderful world of Software!

From 2017-2021, I attended Worcester Polytechnic Institute, where I earned a BS in Computer Science and a BS in Interactive Media and Game Development. Afterwards, I moved to Washington state where I spent 3 years working at the startup HaptX as a Software Engineer. There, I continued to hone my skills with C++ and industry-level Software Engineering.

In my free time I enjoy tinkering with electronics, running, hiking, or just hanging out with my partner and cat watching movies!

Currently I am seeking a Software Engineering role in the Seattle area with a focus on systems or application development using C++ and/or Rust.
