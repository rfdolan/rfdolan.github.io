ABOUT

Hi, my name is Raymond Dolan! I was born and raised in the great little state of Rhode Island. I always knew I wanted to do something challenging but rewarding where I could use my brain to improve people's lives! That's how I ended up pursuing Computer Science and Game Development. From 2017-2021, I attended Worcester Polytechnic Institute, where I earned a BS in Computer Science and a BS in Interactive Media and Game Development. Afterwards, I spent 3 years working at the startup HaptX as a Software Engineer, continuing to hone my skills with C++ and game engines.

Currently I am seeking a Software Engineering role in the Seattle area with a focus on systems or application development using C, C++, or Rust.
